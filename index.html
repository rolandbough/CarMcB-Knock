KNOCK

How to Play

You are in your apartment. Someone is knocking at your door.


Characters:

  • Dave - Your neighbor who lost his keys. Let him in within 10 seconds or you die. (Safe to let in)
  • Mimic - Looks like Dave but has RED EYES. Only visible with light ON. DO NOT let in.
  • Stalker - Black figure with red eyes. Will kill you if you view it with light ON.

Controls:

  • L Key - Toggle light switch (set BEFORE opening peephole)
  • SPACE - Open/Close peephole
  • E Key - Open door (to let visitor in)

IMPORTANT: You must set the light status BEFORE opening the peephole!

Goal: Survive 10 encounters.
